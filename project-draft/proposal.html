Problem Statement

Overview of Food Banks and Food Insecurity

For our final project, we will be targeting the issue of food insecurity in the Seattle area, which leads to individuals not having reliable, quality, and nutritious food. Moreover, various people face food insecurity for different reasons such as economic hardship and social inequality. Seattle in particular has a pressing issue of insecurity due to high living costs and income inequalities. As a result, various food banks are located in the Seattle area to provide for community members who struggle to afford quality groceries. Due to the increased amount of people who require food bank services, food banks face challenges in meeting the high demand.

Identify an information technology problem in that space

A key information technology problem is the lack of centralized information regarding the needs of the food banks and the opportunities for donors to contribute to the needs of the food banks. Currently, food banks struggle to communicate their needs, and donors lack a streamlined process to identify where their contributions are most needed. This often leads to mismatches in donations and shortages of specific items.

At least 1 previous or existing attempt to solve this problem

There have been websites like The Seattle Food Commitee which includes a map for people to to find food banks near them and also gives contact information. However, food banks would not be able to request food items or be able to provide information that they need from users. These could be information such as dates to donate food, contact information, locations, etc.

Through creating this project, we hope to address the issue of food insecurity in the Seattle area and provide low-income individuals with greater access to nutritious food options. Furthermore, we want food banks to be able to request items they need at a certain time, so they can better serve the community with a diverse selection.

App Description

Who are the users of the application?

  • Food bank staff
  • Food bank volunteers
  • Food bank clients
  • People who want to donate
  • People that require food bank services

Our application will have the greatest impact on low-income minority individuals in the Seattle community. Our platform will allow these low-income individuals to have greater access to the meals they need the most.

What will the users do with the web app? What kind of interactive features are you expecting to provide? Remember that you app will need to include at least "two and a half" significant features; be sure to explicitly identify and explain what all of them are!

  • Users that are looking to donate: Be able to access an interactive map of the Seattle area that has pinpoints that show the various food banks in Seattle. The pinpoints will show which items are requested by the food banks.
  • Food banks: An online table that will be able to keep track of the type of items, dates, and locations, contact information that are donated. Be able to request for volunteers.
  • Interaction between Food Banks and Donars:An announcement feature that will allow food banks to communicate with clients and volunteers about what food they are currently lacking and any upcoming events that are being planned or happening.

What kind of information will the users be viewing?

Users will be able to view information from food banks, which will be able to post items that are lacking, allowing users to interact with these posts accordingly. These information will most likely be made up information by our group as we are not able to access real food bank data. Our main goal is display the functions and purpose of this app to the users.

How will using this app help to solve your chosen problem?

To solve the problems mentioned in the problem statement, our platform addresses the critical gap by creating a centralized hub that connects food banks with donors in the Seattle area. Since food banks can post their needs in real time, users can access that information, resulting in more effective donations. In doing so, we are optimizing the distribution of resources and empowering the Seattle community to better support those in need.

What is something that makes your life worse, and that you think that a web app might be able to help with?

One thing that makes our lives worse is not being able to have a good quality, nutritious meal. Our web app will address this problem by allowing food banks to have a more diverse selection of food options, enabling low-income individuals to achieve their proper daily nutritious needs.
